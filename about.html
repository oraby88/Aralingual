About Aralingual

At Aralingual, our journey began with a mission to revolutionize the gaming landscape for Arab gamers. We recognized the immense potential of the Arabic-speaking market and understood the frustrations they faced due to the absence of localized Arabic games. This not only resulted in a subpar gaming experience but also led to piracy and the creation of temporary Arabic versions, ultimately causing a significant loss of players, reputation, and revenue

Driven by our passion for gaming and the desire to bridge this gap, we embarked on a quest to bring the same level of gaming excellence enjoyed by players worldwide to Arabic-speaking countries. We realized the true significance of this market, particularly in the realm of mobile games, where the potential for success is staggering. In fact, Saudi Arabia boasts the highest average revenue per mobile paying user (ARPPU) rate, with an astounding $270 per person, surpassing even the figures in China by 88%.
